       PROCEDURE DIVISION.


       ENTRY-PARA.
           PERFORM MAIN-PARA
           STOP RUN.


       MAIN-PARA.
       PERFORM VARYING i_main FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL NOT (i_main <= 3)
       PERFORM VARYING j_main FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL NOT (j_main <= 3)
       DISPLAY i_main   " x "   j_main   " = "   (i_main * j_main)
       END-PERFORM
       END-PERFORM
       EXIT.
