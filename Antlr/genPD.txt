       PROCEDURE DIVISION.


       ENTRY-PARA.
           PERFORM MAIN-PARA
           STOP RUN.


       MAIN-PARA.
       MOVE 84 TO n_main
       DIVIDE n_main BY 4 GIVING TEMP_0 REMAINDER TEMP_0
       DIVIDE n_main BY 7 GIVING TEMP_1 REMAINDER TEMP_1
       IF TEMP_0 = 0  AND  TEMP_1 = 0
       DISPLAY 1
       ELSE
       DISPLAY 0
       END-IF
       EXIT.
