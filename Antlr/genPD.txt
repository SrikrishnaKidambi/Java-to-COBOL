       PROCEDURE DIVISION.


       MAIN-PARA.
      * Input from ODT is enabled
       DISPLAY "Enter day number (1-7): " WITH NO ADVANCING
       ACCEPT day_main
       EVALUATE day_main
       WHEN 1
       DISPLAY "Monday"
       WHEN 2
       DISPLAY "Tuesday"
       WHEN 3
       DISPLAY "Wednesday"
       WHEN 4
       DISPLAY "Thursday"
       WHEN 5
       DISPLAY "Friday"
       WHEN 6
       DISPLAY "Saturday"
       WHEN 7
       DISPLAY "Sunday"
       WHEN OTHER
       DISPLAY "Invalid day number"
       END-EVALUATE
       STOP RUN.
